3. _spy_
_person who secretly collects information about another country’s government or_
_organisations_

4. _read people_
_ability to understand people’s real intentions through their behaviour and body_
_language_

5. _value (something)_
_consider something important_

6. _reach for the stars_

_(idiom) have high or ambitious aims; try to achieve something difficult even if it_
_seems impossible_
